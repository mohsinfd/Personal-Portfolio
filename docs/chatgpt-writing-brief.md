# ChatGPT Writing Brief: Mohsin Dingankar Portfolio Notes

Use this brief to generate short essays for the "Notes on Product and Systems" section of Mohsin Dingankar's personal portfolio.

## Goal

Write thoughtful, first-person notes that sound like a senior product leader reflecting on real product work. The writing should help readers understand how Mohsin thinks about ambiguous, systems-heavy, 0-to-1 product environments.

## Voice

Calm, precise, personal, and lightly editorial. Avoid hype, generic leadership language, and LinkedIn-style certainty. The writing should feel like an operator memo or field note, not a growth thread.

## Background To Use

Mohsin is a product leader across consumer fintech, gaming, travel, loyalty, and enterprise products. Current focus includes BankKaro and great.cards: credit-card and loan journeys, recommendation systems, API-first product rails, bank/lender integrations, category calculators, and AI-assisted prototyping. Past work includes FanClash, KYC and compliance journeys across 150+ countries, loyalty systems at 700M+ user-profile scale, enterprise SaaS, and founder/operator work in travel.

## Themes To Explore

- Product leadership before the problem is clear
- Why APIs and product contracts are product decisions
- Building first versions in ambiguous environments
- Recommendation systems as decision products, not content pages
- KYC, compliance, and invisible rails as user experience
- What scale teaches you about trust, fallback logic, and operations
- How AI-assisted prototyping changes product discovery without replacing judgment

## Style Rules

- Write in first person.
- Keep each note between 500 and 900 words.
- Use concrete examples from BankKaro, great.cards, FanClash, KYC, loyalty systems, or founder/operator work.
- Avoid phrases like "in today's fast-paced world", "game-changer", "unlock", "seamless", "delve", "leverage" unless they are truly necessary.
- Do not make claims that sound bigger than the evidence.
- End with a practical insight, not a motivational flourish.
